A BRIEF STORY OF MY LIFE

My parents created a beautiful childhood for me— a time that still lives vividly in my memory. As a child, I was restless, emotional, and easily upset, yet in every picture and video, I always held a book and a pen. I didnot know what I wrote back then; I just felt peace when they were beside me

My first day of school was full of excitement. Every year I counted the days, dreaming of reaching 12th grade. My passion grew so strong that I took an exam and moved directly from grade one to grade two, starting fresh with new classmates.

I studied until grade nine, but the situation in the country darkened the future of our generation. I fought hard for my dreams, but in a land where the gun is stronger than the pen, justice belongs to the one holding the weapon, not the one holding the book.

Yet I never danced to the “song of the gunman.” Like a bird who still dreams of flying even inside a cage, and like a parrot who does not stay silent in captivity— I chose not to give up. And today, despite everything, I carry a world of achievements.
